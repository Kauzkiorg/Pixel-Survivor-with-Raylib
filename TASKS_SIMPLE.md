## 🎓 OOP + DSA for Electronics Students

### OOP Concepts
- ✅ **Inheritance**: Entity → Player, Enemy, Skill, Weapon
- ✅ **Polymorphism**: Different skill/weapon types
- ✅ **Encapsulation**: Rarity affects stats internally
- ✅ **Enums**: Rarity, SkillType, WeaponType, Difficulty

### DSA Concepts
- ✅ **Arrays**: Skills[4], Weapons array
- ✅ **Vectors**: Dynamic enemy/bullet/skill storage
- ✅ **Enums**: Discrete level/rarity values
- ✅ **Structs**: Skill stats, Weapon stats, DifficultyStats

---

## 💡 Keep It Simple!

**DON'T**: Make 100 weapons, complex skill interactions, particle effects  
**DO**: Make 4-5 weapons, simple stat multipliers, basic colors

**Each feature should be < 100 lines of code**
